Introduction

Optimized Education is a startup company and experimental project that is aimed to revolutionize education as we know it. At Optimized Education we consider the future of education very important and we are very dedicated to the cause thus continuously researching in the depths of psychology, IT and learning methodologies to be able to provide the best model and system that suits everyone's educational needs.

The vision

Our vision is a world where education and profession does not separate but unite us.

The mission

We at Optimized E-ducation consider it our mission to create a system that unites the different aspect of education, self-development and profession and focuses on the individual but connects them as a whole and making it the standard.
